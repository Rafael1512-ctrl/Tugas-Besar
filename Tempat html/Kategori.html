Bandeng Presto

Bandeng Presto is an Indonesian dish where bandeng is pressure-cooked with spices until its bones become soft, creating a tender, flavorful, and easily edible meal.

Lumpia

Lumpia Semarang is a traditional Indonesian spring roll filled with bamboo shoots, eggs, and shrimp or chicken, served either fresh or deep-fried with a sweet sauce.

Mochi

Mochi Semarang is a traditional Indonesian snack made of glutinous rice dough filled with sweet peanut paste, coated in sesame seeds, and known for its soft, chewy texture.
